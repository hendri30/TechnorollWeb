Design & Features

Shell
The shells are made from special ERW conveyor tubing with close tolerance ensuring smooth, eccentrically free running with minimum diameter runout. Smallest rotating weight of roller require low horsepower, thereby conserving power and energy due to lowest possible dynamic friction.

Bearing Housing
The bearing housing are made of deep drawn quality sheet pressed to high degree of accuracy, keeping tolerance at bearing seating region as specified by the bearing manufactures.

Bearing
Technoroll rollers are fitted with single row deep groove ball bearings, ZZ shielded type, C3 or C4 internal clearance. We have selected these bearings because they give up to ten minutes angular deflection in their operation, coupled with even load distribution within each bearing race.

Shaft
Made from carbon steel round bar. The round bar are cut, turned with perfect tolerance and ground bearing seating to ensure perfect fit between bearing and shaft.

Labyrinth Seal
Time tested and universally accepted multistage sealing arrangement made of nylon are provide on both side of the bearing with dust cover and waterproof rain cap. The components are assembled together with high quality grease packing ensuring effective sealing of bearing from ingress of dust and moisture, giving trouble free long life operation.

Lubrication
All bearings, labyrinth sealing are permanently lubricated.

Testing
All the rollers are thoroughly tested for free rotation, measurement of friction factors, dust and water proof sealing before dispatch.

Techno Pulley

We manufacture conveyor pulley for mining, cement, power plant, coal terminal and various applications in bulk handling. Our mission is to provide quality and yet competitive product to our customers. We can manufacture pulley to your specification and design the pulley for your needs.

All pulley shafts are generally keyed with the hub and equip with RINGFEDER locking assembly. All pulleys are statically balanced. The lagging for drive pulleys are provided by either cold or hot vulcanizing natural rubber with herringbone groove as per customer requirement. Non drive pulleys are provided with plain lagging unless agreed otherwise with the customer. The shore hardiness of rubber lagging are 55 to 65 (durometer) Shore A.

Pulley Locking Assembly

Ringefeder - Keyless locking assembly a famous shrink fit device for quick mounting and dismounting. It has a benefit by avoiding concentrating load at the shaft due to any key machining on the shaft and any regular device of locking element.

Hot Strip Lagging Xtrusion-Lag

Xtrusion-Lag is hot lagging that use automatic rubber extruded strip builder to apply rubber lagging directly on the pulley without the need of hand-stitch. This technology uses an extruded rubber strip or ribbon spirally wrap around the pulley by machine. Traverse or progression of the build is accomplished by either advancing the workpiece or the extruder. Rubber is accumulated by sequential wrap of an extruded rubber ribbon around the pulley. The rubber strip is pressed against the pulley by adjustable hydraulic arm come with rollers to act as stitcher.

Xtrusion-Lag is applied continuously onto pulley surface. The warm rubber strip adhere firmly layer to layer without any need of applying rubber cement, resulting no air blister on the whole lagging process.

About Techno Roll

Technoroll offers a complete range of standard and custom designed conveyor idlers for various bulk handling application in gravel, mining, cement, coal processing, bulk terminal, fertilizer and in-plant conveying applications throughout industry.

Technoroll design and manufacture idlers in different trough angles and for various usages in a wide range of belt conveying. The range include fixed carrying, fixed return, impact idler, training idlers, transition idlers to suit different belt widths.

Technoroll idlers and pulleys pass through a stringent ISO 9001 quality inspection, before they become a key component in your conveyor system.

Technoroll idler also conforms to CEMA (Conveyor Equipment Manufacturers Association) standards, along with other industrial standards, ensuring the best available product on the market today.

Technoroll Range :

  • Trough Idlers
  • Flat Carrying Idlers
  • Flat Return Idlers
  • Impact Idlers
  • Training Idlers
  • V - Return Idlers
  • Picking and Feeding Idlers

Belt Widths : Full range from 400mm to 2,400mm
